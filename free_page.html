Famous DJs and Music Producers

Here are some of the DJs and music producers who inspire me in my musical journey.

Martin Garrix

Martin Garrix is a Dutch DJ and producer, known for hits like "Animals" and "Scared to Be Lonely".

Avicii

Avicii was a Swedish DJ and producer whose music, including "Wake Me Up", changed the EDM scene forever.

David Guetta

David Guetta, a French DJ and producer, is known for his collaborations with top artists and hits like "Titanium".

Alan Walker

Alan Walker, a British-Norwegian DJ, gained fame with "Faded" and is known for his unique style and masks.

Calvin Harris

Calvin Harris is a Scottish DJ and producer, renowned for hits like "Summer" and "Feel So Close".
